Why choose to Walk?

I usually walk when I have the time and the weather is nice.

Walking to school takes me 20 minutes so when I had the time I would walk. I didnt run into issues when walking. If I walked this allowed me to have the time to talk on the phone, listen to music and in general enjoy the realxing walk (get some excersice). The times I would opt out of a walk was when I had exactly 20 minutes to get to school or any other place I needed to be at a specific time. When I knew I couldnt stroll and not rush I usually opted out of walking. Me being able bodied allowed me to have the choice of either walking or taking another form of transportation.
