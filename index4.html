Why choose the subway?

I usually rode the subway when it was raining or I had no other option.

Instances where I had no simple choice but to take the subway was when I would go to work in brooklyn, I had no other normal option that wouldnt take over an hour to get there and cost me more than 20 dollars. I of course did have the option of taking an uber but my budget did not allow for such a decision. While the subway is comfortable it is not great for transporting materials on day 2 of analyzing my mobility choices I had a project that was too big for the turnstiles, the subway exit gate was manned by policeman. The only way I could get through was to ask for permission to pass. This created a not pleasent experiance because I felt I shouldnt need to ask for permission to use a public transport I am paying for. Imagining a somone who might have a baby carrier with them or a disability that requires them to use the exit gate showed me that while I might have been bothere for only that instance some people while using the subway constantly need to ask for permission use something as basic as a door. Another reason I might not opt for the subway is when I have a bus avalible and I have more time. This is because while the bus might take longer to get me to my destination usually its less crowded.
